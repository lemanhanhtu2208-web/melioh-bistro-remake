The Story

MeliOh Bistro is a romantic dining destination in Da Nang, created for candlelight dinners, anniversaries, birthdays and proposal moments by the Han River. Since 2021, MeliOh has served food, harmony and laughter through warm hospitality, elegant ambiance and fusion cuisine.
