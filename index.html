Welcome to MeliOh

A romantic bistro for moments
worth remembering.

MeliOh Bistro brings together warm hospitality, an elegant ambiance and a fusion of local and international cuisine, crafted for couples looking for a memorable dining experience in Da Nang.

At the heart of it sits our glass dome, the Mái vòm tình yêu, glowing over candlelit tables and the river beyond.
